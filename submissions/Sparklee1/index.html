Royale Gorge

On July 10, 2016 my family and I visited the Royale Gorge Bridge and Park in Canon City, CO. The Royale Gorge Bridge is one of the most famous suspension bridges in the world. The bridge sets 956 feet above the Arkansas River, one of the United States longest rivers.
