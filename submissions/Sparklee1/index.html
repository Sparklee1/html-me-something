Royal Gorge

On July 10, 2016 my family and I visited the Royal Gorge Bridge and Park in Canon City, CO. The Royal Gorge Bridge is one of the most famous suspension bridges in the world. The bridge sets 956 feet above the Arkansas River, one of the United States longest rivers.

Garden of the Gods

On July 11, 2016 my family and I ventured into the Garden of the Gods in Colorado Springs, CO. The Garden of the Gods' formations of red rocks were created during a geological upheval along a natural fault line over a million years ago. It was an amazing place for us to hike and take in the beauty of Colorado

Pikes Peak

On July 12, 2016 my family took the cog railway up the mountain to Pikes Peak, Manitou Springs, Co. Pikes Peak is located in the Rocky Mountains and is one of most famous summits in the United States. The peak is 14,115 ft above sea level and the temperature at the summit can be 30 to 40 degrees colder than Manitou Springs

Seven Falls

Seven Falls is located in Colorado Springs, CO and on July 13, 2016 my family decides to see the georgeous natural wonder. Here we viewed striking rock formations and the magnificent series of waterfalls as we climbed the 224 steps to hiking trails at the top.

Red Rocks Ampitheatre

21 Pilots played at Red Rocks Ampitheatre on July 13, 2016 and my Son and I got to enjoy the show in this breathtaking open-air ampitheatre.
